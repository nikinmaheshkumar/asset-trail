## Design Context

### Users
- Primary users: club admins.
- Context: mostly managing inventory, approvals, and audit/history on laptops.

### Use Cases
- Approve loan requests quickly.
- Audit/track history (who has what, due dates, activity logs).
- Manage inventory (add/edit items, stock, status, location).
- Manage users/roles.
- Borrow items quickly when needed.

### Brand Personality
- Technical / Precise / Calm.
- Goal: high clarity and low noise; trustworthy and efficient.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Calm, utilitarian admin tool polish.
- Prioritize legibility, density, and predictable interactions over novelty.

### Quality Bar
- MVP polish (ship today): fix inconsistencies, a11y, interaction states, spacing, and copy with minimal-risk changes.

### Design Principles
- Consistency first: shared patterns for tables, filters, badges, and headers.
- Calm hierarchy: strong titles, quiet metadata, clear status semantics.
- Fast admin workflows: reduce scanning effort; emphasize state and next actions.
- Accessible by default: keyboard, focus visibility, contrast (WCAG AA where feasible).
